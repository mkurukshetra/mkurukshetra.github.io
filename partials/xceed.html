XCEED LEARN ABOUT XCEED

Kurukshetra is not a confined battle; it expands its horizon to the unprecedented limits of the world. Ever thought of backing out of the Battle of Brains, just because the geographic barriers stopped you? Are you one of those longing to participate in one of the largest techno management festivals of the country, but are not able to? Fret not! XCEED is here.

Xceed is the outreach event of Kurukshetra, which is being conducted in various parts of the country and beyond. This encourages the technical spark of the student community and enthuse the innovative excellence in them.

FAQs:

1) How is Xceed different from Kurukshetra?

Xceed is very much a part of Kurukshetra that serves as the precursor to the main event; it is a mini Kurukshetra of sorts.

2) When does Xceed usually happen?

Xceed is usually conducted at various institutions between 30 and 60 days before Kurukshetra.

3) What kinds of events are conducted in Xceed?

The events organized would be Robotics based onsite events, quizzes of various genres and online programming contest. Workshops on the various cutting edge technologies and latest developments will also be conducted.

4) How are the participants of Xceed benefitted?

The winners of the Xceed events conducted elsewhere are given a direct chance to participate in the Grand Finale of the same event conducted at Kuruskehtra. Further, there are also exciting cash awards and certificates, which speak for themselves.
